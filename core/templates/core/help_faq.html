Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Login or register as a citizen, then click "Report Emergency" or "Disaster Reports" to submit a new disaster report with details and location.

Go to "Request Aid" from the home page or dashboard, fill in the required details, and submit your request. Authorities will review and respond.

Register as a volunteer, then update your skills and availability. You will be contacted or assigned to tasks as needed by authorities.

Only users with the "Authority" role or superusers can access the admin dashboard and manage disaster response operations.

Use the "Contact Us" form on the home page. Your message will be sent to the DRIS support team for follow-up.
